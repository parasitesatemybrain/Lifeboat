Thermometer and Heat pack

 

Entonox is made up of 50% Nitrous Oxide and 50% Oxygen, and at temperatures below 50C they separate.  Therefore the thermometer can aid on knowing when to need to re-mix the contents together.  To re-mix the gases, the cylinder can be re-warmed by wrapping the heat pack around the cylinder.  It is also a good idea to invert the cylinder a few times to ensure mixing.
